About Me

Howdy! My name is Brian Polasek and I am a software engineer at Tempset Controls.


I am seeking a career in the tech field in Austin, Texas. I developed my strong work ethic growing up and helping out on a ranch in Hutto, Texas. I have a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from Texas A&M University and minors in Cybersecurity and Mathematics. My interests include cybersecurity, machine learning, human-computer interaction, IoT and cloud networks. I am addicted to learning new things, listening to podcasts, creating music and have traveled to 11 countries so far (with hopes for more!). Other passions include home tech projects, such as a home server and weather station, as well as home brewing mead and kombucha.


This website will be a a showcase for my current and past projects, as well as information for a future meadery.
